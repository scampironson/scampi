Scampi's Daily Schedule:

Time Activity
8:00 AM Morning Walk
8:30 AM Breakfast
12:00 PM Afternoon Walk
5:00 PM Evening Walk
6:30 PM Dinner
10:00 Night Walk
10:30 Crate/sleep time

Scampi's Morning Routine:

  • Open the door to the crate
  • Scampi slowly stretches as he walks out of the crate
  • Scampi wags his tail and shakes his whole body
  • Scampi flops over to his back with paws up, ready for pets
  • Scampi will reposition himself in a lap, or on top of feet, to continue getting pets
  • Eventually it’s time to put on his collar, harness, and leash
  • It’s time to go for his first walk of the morning

Scampi's Food:

  • 2x a day Scampi gets ½ cup of kibble
  • The kibble goes into a red treat dispensing ball if he has a lot of energy or needs to be distracted
  • It can also go directly in his food bowl. Add a spoongul of pumpkin puree as desired
  • Scampi also gets treats during the day to help with training

Scampi's Training:

  • Scampi completed Basic Manners Class at Doggie Academy
  • Some commands he knows are:
  • Sit
  • Down
  • Come
  • Place
  • Attention walking
  • His training is ongoing
