Scampi’s Favorite Brooklyn Activities

(So far)

  • Herbert Von King Dog Park
    • Scampi loves getting all of the other dogs to run around and chase him. He is not afraid of getting knocked around a little
    • He would love to visit everyday
    • Because the ground is loose dirt and wood chips he gets dirty immediately and usually needs a bath after visiting
  • Fort Greene Farmers market
    • There are a lot of people and dogs to meet
    • Scampi can sometimes get a little overwhelmed by all the smells and the crowd, but with breaks to rest he does really well
  • Visiting the dry cleaner
    • No one working there has ever given Scampi a treat or pet him, but he always gets s excited when we walk by, and wants to go in
  • Saying hi at Who's Your Doggy
    • This is our local pet store and they have a great stock of supplies, toys and treats, as well as offering grooming services
    • Scampi always gets treats and pets when we go here
    • It can be hard to get him to leave when we are done
  • Going to the vet
    • Most dogs are afraid of the vet, but Scampi gets a lot of attention and unlimited treats from the vet and their staff
    • He is so excited the whole time we are there I don’t think he even realizes when he gets shots
  • Getting tacos at Chilo’s
    • Scampi did not get to eat any tacos, so maybe this wasn’t really his favorite activity, but I had fun
    • He was able to lay on the floor and be calm while I enjoyed my taco and margarita
